NoteSpend

An expense tracker built for people who think in notes, not complicated spreadsheets.

Download on the App Store

About NoteSpend

Instead of asking you to log transactions by manually filling in fields, dropdowns, and rigid categories, NoteSpend lets you write naturally—like you already do in your journal.

Write anything: a coffee, a trip plan, or random thoughts. If money shows up, NoteSpend understands it. Expenses are quietly picked up in the background without forcing structure or interrupting your flow. Start messy, stay clear.

Core Features

  • No Form Friction: Type freely in a simple daily note. Mix expenses, thoughts, and plans in the absolute same place.
  • Life Tags: Use tags like #japantrip or #houserenovation to group spending by projects or phases of your life.
  • Quiet Intelligence: Notes are automatically parsed into transactions. Recurring expenses are detected seamlessly.
