Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 30, 2026

TL;DR: Your financial data belongs to you. NoteSpend stores your transactions locally on your device. When you use AI features, your text is processed anonymously and discarded immediately. We do not sell your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Data

  • Usage Statistics: We track anonymous usage counts (e.g., "50 AI requests made") to enforce subscription quotas and preventing abuse.
  • Device Identifier: We use an anonymous identifier derived from your iCloud account (CloudKit User Record ID) to sync your Pro subscription status across devices.
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash logs provided by Apple to help us fix bugs.

1.2 Data Processed via AI

When you type a note (e.g., "Sushi $40"), the text is sent to our secure backend.

  • Processor: We use Google Gemini (via Google Cloud/Cloudflare) to parse your text.
  • Retention: Your text is processed in memory and discarded immediately after the response is generated. We do not use your financial data to train AI models.

2. Where Your Data Lives

  • Financial Data: Your journals, transactions, and budgets are stored locally on your device.
  • iCloud Sync (Pro): If you are a Pro subscriber, your data syncs via Apple's CloudKit. This data is encrypted by Apple and accessible only by you. We cannot see it.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide the Service (parsing your notes into transactions).
  • To manage your subscription status (Free vs. Pro).
  • To enforce fair usage limits preventing API abuse.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Data is only shared with service providers necessary to run the app:

  • Google (Gemini): For AI text parsing.
  • Cloudflare: For secure API infrastructure and rate limiting.
  • Apple: For payment processing and iCloud sync.

5. Your Rights

Since your data is stored locally or in your private iCloud:

  • Access/Export: You can view all your data within the app.
  • Delete: Deleting the app/data from your device enables you to remove your local data. You can manage iCloud data via your Apple ID settings.

6. Contact

If you have any questions, please contact us at support@notespend.app.
