Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 June 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Focus Search handles your data.

1. Data Collection

Focus Search does not collect any personal information or usage data. All data related to your searches, preferences, and saved links is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

2. Third-Party Services

Focus Search does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services. Your activity within the app remains entirely private and confined to your device.

3. App Redirection Disclaimer

Focus Search helps users open third-party apps (e.g., YouTube, Reddit, Instagram) directly with search terms. We are not affiliated with or responsible for the content, functionality, or data practices of any third-party apps. If a third-party app discontinues or alters its deep linking behavior, Focus Search may not function as expected. We are not liable for such disruptions but will try to accommodate reasonable updates when feasible.

4. Payments

Focus Search is available as a one-time purchase of $5.99. Payments and refunds are processed securely through Apple's App Store. Focus Search does not handle or store any payment information. Please refer to Apple's terms and policies for payment processing, refunds, and eligibility.

5. Data Security

Because all data is stored only on your device, you are responsible for the security of your device and any data stored within Focus Search. We do not have access to your data and cannot retrieve it if your device is lost, damaged, or reset.

6. Children's Privacy

Focus Search is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has submitted data through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Any significant updates will be communicated through the app where applicable. Continued use of Focus Search after such changes constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
